# Vendor Due Diligence

## Purpose

Vendor due diligence is required before onboarding third parties that may access company systems, confidential data, or regulated information.

## Recordkeeping

The due diligence package must be retained with the vendor record for audit review.

## Review Areas

- Security controls and certifications
- Privacy and data processing terms
- Financial stability and sanctions screening
- Subprocessor and hosting model transparency
