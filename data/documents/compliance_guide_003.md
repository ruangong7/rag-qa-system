# Breach Notification

## Control Objective

This procedure defines when and how a personal data breach must be reported to regulators and affected parties.

## Governance

The DBRT coordinates the legal, security, and communications response and maintains the notification record.

## Notification Rules

- Notify the supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of a reportable breach.
- Notify affected data subjects without undue delay when the breach presents a high risk to their rights and freedoms.

## Required Inputs

- Summary of the incident
- Categories of affected data
- Estimated number of affected subjects
- Containment and remediation status
