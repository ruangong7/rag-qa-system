# Audit Evidence

## Acceptable Evidence Types

- System screenshots with timestamps
- Exported reports from systems of record
- Approval logs and ticket history
- Policy documents and change records

## Evidence Quality Standard

Evidence must be complete, legible, time-bound, and traceable to the relevant control objective.

## Purpose

This document defines the standards for collecting and preserving evidence used in internal and external audits.

## Retention

Audit evidence must be retained according to the applicable control schedule and made available on request during audit review.
