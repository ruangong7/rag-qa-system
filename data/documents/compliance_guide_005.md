# Cross-Border Transfer

## Evidence

Keep the transfer assessment, approval record, and supporting contract documents in the compliance repository.

## Policy Statement

Cross-border transfer of personal data must be reviewed before data is moved outside the originating jurisdiction.

## Required Controls

- Approved contractual transfer mechanism
- Transfer impact assessment
- Validation of destination-country risk
- Additional approval for sensitive personal data where applicable

## Review Standard

The data owner, privacy team, and legal team must confirm that the transfer purpose is legitimate and proportionate.
