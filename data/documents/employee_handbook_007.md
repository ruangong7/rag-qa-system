# New Hire Checklist

## Before Day One

- Confirm signed offer and employment documents.
- Create user accounts and access credentials.
- Prepare equipment and workstation assignment.

## Completion Standard


The onboarding checklist is considered complete only after all required tasks are marked closed in the designated system.

## First Week Tasks


- Complete HR orientation.
- Review security and confidentiality policies.
- Enroll in mandatory training.
- Meet the manager and key cross-functional partners.

## Objective

This checklist ensures that each new employee completes the required onboarding steps in a consistent order.

## Systems Involved

- Workday for employee records
- Feishu HR for onboarding workflow
- IT service desk for device and account provisioning
