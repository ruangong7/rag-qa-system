# Privacy Request

## Applicable Regulations

This procedure governs the handling of data subject requests and is intended to support compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and related privacy regulations.

## Audit Evidence

- Identity verification records
- Case communications
- Final resolution records

## Fact Anchors

- primary_fact: data subject request
- required_value: 30 calendar days
- evidence: identity verification
- owner: Legal Counsel
- systems: LegalHold Tracker, PrivacyOps Queue

## Out of Scope

This procedure does not apply to API implementation details or service topology design discussions.

## Regulatory Review Narrative

The control objective is to ensure that every valid data subject request is processed within 30 calendar days and supported by effective identity verification.

## Role Responsibilities

- Business team: register the request case.
- Compliance team: review, validate identity, and process the request.
- Legal Counsel: oversee the workflow and ensure legal compliance.

## Systems of Record

- LegalHold Tracker for intake and case management
- PrivacyOps Queue for task assignment and tracking
