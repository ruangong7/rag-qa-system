# Conflict of Interest / 利益冲突

## Examples / 示例

- Undisclosed financial interest in a vendor
- 未披露的供应商财务利益关系
- Hiring or approving close relatives without disclosure
- 未披露情况下招聘或审批近亲属
- Personal benefit from company decisions
- 因公司决策获得个人利益

## Policy Statement / 政策声明

Employees must disclose actual, potential, or perceived conflicts of interest.
员工必须披露实际、潜在或可被感知的利益冲突。

## Review / 审核

Disclosures are reviewed by HR, Legal, or Compliance depending on the case.
披露事项由 HR、法务或合规根据情形进行审核。
