# Employee Recognition / 员工表彰

## Governance / 治理要求
Recognition decisions should be documented and aligned with fair and transparent criteria.
表彰决定应记录在案，并符合公平透明的标准。

## Objective / 目标
The employee recognition program acknowledges strong performance and exemplary collaboration.
员工表彰项目用于认可优秀绩效和协作表现。

## Recognition Types / 表彰类型

- Spot awards for exceptional contributions
- 对突出贡献的即时奖励
- Team awards for cross-functional delivery
- 对跨团队交付成果的团队奖励
- Annual recognition for sustained impact
- 对长期影响力的年度表彰
