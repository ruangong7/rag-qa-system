# Sick Leave Certificate

## Exception Handling


- If the employee cannot obtain a medical certificate immediately, the employee must notify HR and explain the situation.
- Extended leave beyond 5 days must be handled under the long-term medical leave policy.

## Fact Anchors


- primary_fact: paid sick leave
- required_value: 5 days
- evidence: medical certificate
- systems: Concur, Finance Portal
- owner: People Operations

## Manager Review Standards


- Verify that the requested duration does not exceed the annual 5-day paid sick leave allowance.
- Review the uploaded medical certificate for completeness and validity.
- Approve or reject the request based on policy compliance.

## Not Applicable To


This policy does not apply to customer ticket routing or production incident handling workflows.

## Policy Summary


Employees may apply for paid sick leave when they are unable to work due to illness. Each employee is entitled to up to 5 days of paid sick leave per year. A valid medical certificate is required as supporting evidence.

## Pre-Submission Checklist


- Confirm that you are a full-time employee.
- Confirm that your annual paid sick leave balance has not been exhausted.
- Prepare a medical certificate issued by a hospital or clinic.
- Log in to the Concur system and complete the leave request form.

## System of Record

- All sick leave requests and status updates are recorded in Concur.
- Payroll-related adjustments are synchronized to the Finance Portal when necessary.
