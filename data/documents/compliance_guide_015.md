# Record Retention Schedule / 记录保留周期

## Example Rules / 示例规则

- Financial records: 10 years
- 财务记录：10 年
- Customer contracts: 7 years
- 客户合同：7 年
- Security logs: 1 year unless a longer period is required
- 安全日志：通常 1 年，除非另有更长期限要求

## Exception Management / 例外管理

Any deviation requires documented approval from the record owner and Compliance.
任何偏离都必须获得记录负责人和合规团队的书面批准。

## Policy Goal / 政策目标

Records must be retained for a period that satisfies legal, regulatory, operational, and audit requirements.
记录必须保留至满足法律、监管、运营和审计要求的期限。
