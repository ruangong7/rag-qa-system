# Data Minimization

## Control Ownership

Business owners, product owners, and privacy reviewers share responsibility for enforcing data minimization.

## Control Requirements

- Define the business purpose before collection.
- Limit fields to those required for the purpose.
- Review periodic reports for overcollection.
- Remove or anonymize data that is no longer needed.

## Principle

Only the minimum personal data necessary for the stated business purpose may be collected, stored, processed, or shared.
