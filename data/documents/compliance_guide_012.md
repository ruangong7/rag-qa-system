# Password Standard / 密码标准

## Additional Control / 附加控制

MFA is required for critical systems wherever technically supported.
只要技术上支持，关键系统必须启用多因素认证。

## Baseline Requirement / 基线要求

Passwords must meet the corporate complexity and rotation requirements.
密码必须符合公司复杂度与轮换要求。

## Minimum Rules / 最低规则

- At least 12 characters
- 至少 12 个字符
- Include upper case, lower case, numbers, and special characters
- 包含大写、小写、数字和特殊字符
- No reuse of recent passwords
- 不得重复使用近期密码
