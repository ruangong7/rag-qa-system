# Access Review / 访问权限复核

## Evidence / 审计证据

Approved review records and remediation actions must be retained.
已批准的复核记录和整改动作必须留存。

## Minimum Frequency / 最低频率


High-risk systems must be reviewed quarterly.
高风险系统必须每季度复核一次。
Other business systems must be reviewed at least semi-annually.
其他业务系统至少每半年复核一次。

## Objective / 目标

Periodic access review ensures that users retain only the permissions necessary for their current job duties.
定期访问权限复核确保用户仅保留履行当前职责所需的权限。
