# Travel Expense

## Approval Flow


The direct manager reviews policy compliance first, and Finance performs the final reimbursement check.

## Non-Reimbursable Items


Personal entertainment, unapproved upgrades, and undocumented cash expenses are not reimbursable.

## Reimbursable Categories


- Airfare and train tickets
- Hotel accommodation within policy limits
- Local transportation for business purposes
- Meals within approved daily allowance

## Scope


This policy governs reimbursement for approved business travel expenses.

## Submission Requirements


- Submit the claim in Concur within 10 business days after travel completion.
- Attach itemized receipts for all reimbursable expenses.
- Provide business purpose and trip dates.
