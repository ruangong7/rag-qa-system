# Consent Management

## Audit Expectations

Compliance reviewers must be able to trace how consent was collected and whether downstream systems honor withdrawal requests.

## Objective

Consent management ensures that personal data processing based on consent is traceable, revocable, and limited to the authorized purpose.

## Operational Requirements

- Record the time, method, and scope of consent.
- Link consent to the relevant processing purpose.
- Provide a practical withdrawal mechanism.
- Stop consent-based processing after a valid withdrawal request is confirmed.
