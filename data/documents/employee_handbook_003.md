# Remote Work Eligibility

## Boundary Conditions


- Certain roles may require stricter on-site coverage.
- Remote employees must use the corporate VPN and comply with security requirements.

## Common Errors


- Employees requesting remote work before probation is complete.
- Managers approving requests without documenting the decision.

## Employee FAQ


**Q1: Am I eligible for remote work?**
A1: Yes, employees who have completed probation may request up to 3 days of remote work per week.

**Q2: How do I submit a remote work request?**
A2: Submit the request in Slack with your work plan and justification, and route it to your manager for approval.

**Q3: Do I need to provide additional proof?**
A3: In most cases, no. The HR system already records your start date and probation completion date.

## Fact Anchors


- primary_fact: remote work
- required_value: 3 days per week
- eligibility: after probation
- system: Slack

## HRBP Recordkeeping


- Capture the request date, approval date, and final decision.
- Update the employee record to reflect remote work status.
- Review approved remote work arrangements quarterly.

## Scope


This policy applies to full-time employees who have completed probation. It does not apply to production incident handling or API schema approval activities.
