# Third-Party Risk / 第三方风险

## Review Expectation / 审核要求

Higher-risk third parties require enhanced due diligence and periodic reassessment.
高风险第三方需要增强尽调和定期复评。

## Risk Factors / 风险因素

- Access to confidential or regulated data
- 是否接触机密或受监管数据
- Hosting or processing critical workloads
- 是否承载或处理关键工作负载
- Dependency on subcontractors
- 是否依赖分包商

## Scope / 适用范围

Third-party risk management applies to vendors, contractors, service providers, and other external partners.
第三方风险管理适用于供应商、承包商、服务提供商及其他外部合作方。
