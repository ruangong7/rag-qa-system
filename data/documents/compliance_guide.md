# Compliance Guide — 合规指南 v2024.1

## Part I: Data Protection / 数据保护

### Article 1: General Principles / 总则
All data processing activities must comply with applicable data protection laws
including the Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL) of the People's Republic of China,
the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) of the European Union,
and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

所有数据处理活动必须遵守适用的数据保护法律，包括《中华人民共和国个人信息保护法》(PIPL)、
欧盟《通用数据保护条例》(GDPR) 和加州消费者隐私法案 (CCPA)。

### Article 2: Lawful Basis for Processing / 处理的合法基础
Data processing must have at least one lawful basis:
1. Consent of the data subject (数据主体同意)
2. Performance of a contract (履行合同)
3. Compliance with legal obligation (法律义务)
4. Vital interests (重大利益)
5. Legitimate interests (合法利益)

### Article 3: Data Subject Rights / 数据主体权利
Data subjects have the following rights:
- Right to access (访问权): Obtain confirmation of whether personal data is being processed
- Right to rectification (更正权): Request correction of inaccurate personal data
- Right to erasure (删除权): Request deletion of personal data under certain conditions
- Right to data portability (可携带权): Receive personal data in a structured, machine-readable format
- Right to object (反对权): Object to processing based on legitimate interests

Exercise of rights must be responded to within 30 calendar days.
权利行使请求必须在 30 个自然日内响应。

### Article 4: Data Breach Response / 数据泄露响应
In the event of a personal data breach, the following steps must be taken:
1. Containment (遏制): Immediately isolate affected systems to prevent further data loss.
2. Assessment (评估): Determine the scope, cause, and nature of the breach within 4 hours.
3. Notification (通知): Notify the supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach.
4. Communication (沟通): Inform affected data subjects without undue delay if the breach poses high risk.
5. Remediation (修复): Implement corrective measures and document lessons learned.

Data Breach Response Team (DBRT) must be convened within 1 hour of breach detection.
数据泄露应急响应小组 (DBRT) 必须在发现泄露后 1 小时内召集。

### Article 5: Cross-Border Data Transfer / 数据跨境传输
Cross-border transfer of personal data requires:
- Standard contractual clauses (SCCs) approved by regulatory authorities
- Data transfer impact assessment (DTIA)
- Explicit consent from data subjects for sensitive personal data
- Registration with the Cyberspace Administration of China (CAC) for certain volumes

## Part II: Financial Compliance / 财务合规

### Article 6: Anti-Money Laundering (AML) / 反洗钱
All transactions exceeding RMB 50,000 (or equivalent in foreign currency) must be
screened against sanctions lists maintained by:
- United Nations Security Council
- Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC)
- People's Bank of China (PBOC)

Suspicious transactions must be reported within 24 hours to the compliance officer.
可疑交易必须在 24 小时内向合规官报告。

### Article 7: Record Keeping / 记录保存
Financial records must be retained for a minimum of 10 years from the end of the fiscal year.
财务记录必须从财政年度结束后至少保存 10 年。

## Part III: Export Control / 出口管制

### Article 8: Controlled Technologies / 受控技术
The following technologies require export license before transfer outside China:
- Encryption algorithms with key length > 256 bits
- AI training frameworks capable of distributed training across > 1000 GPUs
- Quantum computing simulators with > 50 qubits

以下技术在向中国境外转移前需要出口许可证：
- 密钥长度超过 256 位的加密算法
- 能够跨 1000 个以上 GPU 进行分布式训练的 AI 训练框架
- 超过 50 量子比特的量子计算模拟器

### Article 9: Sanctions Screening / 制裁筛查
All business partners and customers must be screened against applicable sanctions lists
before engagement. Re-screening must occur at least quarterly.
所有商业伙伴和客户在合作前必须进行制裁名单筛查，至少每季度重新筛查一次。
