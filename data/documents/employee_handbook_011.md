# Performance Improvement Plan / 绩效改进计划

## 1. Manager documents performance concerns and expected outcomes.


1. Manager documents performance concerns and expected outcomes.
1. 经理记录绩效问题与预期改进结果。

## 2. HR reviews the case for policy consistency.


2. HR reviews the case for policy consistency.
2. HR 审核案例是否符合制度要求。

## 3. Employee receives the written plan and review schedule.


3. Employee receives the written plan and review schedule.
3. 员工收到书面计划与复盘安排。

## 4. Final outcome is recorded as successful completion, extension, or separation.


4. Final outcome is recorded as successful completion, extension, or separation.
4. 最终结果记录为完成、延期或解除劳动关系。

## Purpose / 目的


This document defines the process for placing an employee on a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP).
本文档定义员工进入绩效改进计划（PIP）的流程。

## Standard Timeline / 标准周期


- Initial review period: 30 calendar days
- 初始观察周期：30 个自然日
- Extension only with HR approval
- 仅可在 HR 批准后延期

## When It Applies / 适用场景

A PIP may be initiated when performance gaps are sustained and have been documented by the manager.
当员工存在持续性的绩效差距，且经理已经完成记录时，可以启动 PIP。
