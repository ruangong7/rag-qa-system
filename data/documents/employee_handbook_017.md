# Equipment Assignment / 设备发放

## 1. IT prepares equipment based on role requirements.


1. IT prepares equipment based on role requirements.
1. IT 根据岗位需求准备设备。

## 2. The employee signs the asset receipt record.


2. The employee signs the asset receipt record.
2. 员工签署资产领取记录。

## 3. Asset ownership and return obligations are explained during onboarding.


3. Asset ownership and return obligations are explained during onboarding.
3. 入职时说明资产归属和归还义务。

## Purpose / 目的


This policy defines how company laptops, phones, and accessories are assigned and tracked.
本政策定义公司笔记本、手机及配件的发放与追踪方式。

## Return Requirement / 归还要求


All assigned equipment must be returned during offboarding unless otherwise approved.
除另有批准外，离职时必须归还所有已发放设备。
