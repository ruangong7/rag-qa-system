# Information Security Policy / 信息安全政策 ISMS-2024

## 1. Access Control / 访问控制

### 1.1 Principle of Least Privilege / 最小权限原则
Users shall be granted the minimum level of access necessary to perform their job functions.
Access rights must be reviewed quarterly by department managers.
用户应仅被授予执行工作职能所需的最低访问权限。
访问权限必须由部门经理每季度审核一次。

### 1.2 Account Lifecycle / 账号生命周期
- Provisioning: New accounts created within 1 business day of approved request
- Modification: Role changes applied within 4 hours
- Deprovisioning: Accounts disabled within 1 hour of termination notice
- Quarterly access review by managers required

## 2. Incident Response / 事件响应

### 2.1 Severity Classification / 严重性分类

| Severity | Definition | Response Time | Escalation |
|----------|------------|---------------|------------|
| Critical | Data breach, system compromise | 15 minutes | CISO + CEO |
| High | Malware detected, unauthorized access | 1 hour | CISO |
| Medium | Suspicious activity, policy violation | 4 hours | Security Team Lead |
| Low | Minor anomalies, routine alerts | 24 hours | On-call Engineer |

### 2.2 Incident Response Playbook / 事件响应手册

For Critical/High severity incidents:
1. Declare incident and notify DBRT (Data Breach Response Team)
2. Isolate affected systems (network segmentation or shutdown)
3. Preserve evidence (disk images, logs, memory dumps)
4. Perform root cause analysis
5. Implement remediation
6. Post-incident review within 5 business days

## 3. Data Backup & Recovery / 数据备份与恢复

### 3.1 Backup Policy / 备份策略
- Full backup: Weekly (retained 90 days)
- Incremental backup: Daily (retained 30 days)
- Transaction logs: Continuous (retained 7 days)
- Backups encrypted with AES-256 and stored in geographically separate locations

### 3.2 Recovery Testing / 恢复测试
Recovery drills must be conducted quarterly.
RTO verification: Full system recovery within 4 hours.
RPO verification: Maximum data loss not exceeding 15 minutes.
