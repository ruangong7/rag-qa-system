# Business Travel Safety / 差旅安全

## Overview / 概述

Employees traveling for business must follow the company travel safety guidelines.
员工因公出差时必须遵守公司的差旅安全指引。

## Ownership / 责任归属

The employee, manager, and security team share responsibility for travel risk management.
员工、经理和安全团队共同承担差旅风险管理责任。

## Required Actions / 必要动作

- Keep emergency contact information current.
- 保持紧急联系人信息最新。
- Follow destination-specific risk guidance.
- 遵守目的地风险提示。
- Report major safety incidents immediately.
- 重大安全事件须立即上报。
