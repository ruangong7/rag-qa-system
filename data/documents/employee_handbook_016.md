# Workplace Conduct / 职场行为规范

## Principle / 原则

All employees are expected to maintain a respectful, safe, and professional work environment.
所有员工都应维护尊重、安全、专业的工作环境。

## Prohibited Behavior / 禁止行为

- Harassment, discrimination, or retaliation
- 骚扰、歧视或报复行为
- Threats, intimidation, or violence
- 威胁、恐吓或暴力行为
- Misuse of company resources for abusive conduct
- 利用公司资源实施不当行为

## Reporting Channel / 举报渠道

Concerns may be reported to HR, Ethics, or the designated whistleblower channel.
相关问题可向 HR、合规伦理渠道或指定举报渠道反馈。
