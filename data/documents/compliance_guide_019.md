# Privacy Impact Assessment / 隐私影响评估

## Output / 产出物

The assessment must document the processing purpose, risk analysis, mitigation measures, and approval result.
评估结果必须记录处理目的、风险分析、缓解措施和审批结论。

## Purpose / 目的

A privacy impact assessment evaluates whether a new process, feature, or system introduces material privacy risk.
隐私影响评估用于判断新流程、功能或系统是否引入实质性隐私风险。

## When Required / 何时需要

- New collection of personal data
- 新增个人信息收集
- New sharing with third parties
- 新增向第三方共享
- Material change in processing purpose
- 处理目的发生重大变化
