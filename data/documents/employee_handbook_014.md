# Internal Transfer / 内部转岗

## Documentation / 文档要求

Transfer decisions must be reflected in the HRIS and employee file.
转岗决定必须同步到 HRIS 和员工档案中。

## Minimum Conditions / 基本条件


- The employee must be in good standing.
- 员工当前绩效状态需正常。
- The current and receiving managers must both approve.
- 当前经理与接收经理都必须批准。
- HR must confirm job level and compensation alignment.
- HR 必须确认职级与薪酬匹配。

## Objective / 目标

This guideline governs employee transfers between teams, functions, or business units.
本指南规范员工在团队、职能或业务单元之间的内部转岗。
