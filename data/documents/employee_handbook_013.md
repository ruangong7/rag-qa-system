# Overtime Approval / 加班审批

## 1. Employee submits an overtime request with business justification.


1. Employee submits an overtime request with business justification.
1. 员工提交包含业务理由的加班申请。

## 2. Manager reviews workload necessity and approves or rejects.


2. Manager reviews workload necessity and approves or rejects.
2. 经理评估工作必要性并批准或拒绝。

## 3. HR or payroll records approved overtime where applicable.


3. HR or payroll records approved overtime where applicable.
3. 如适用，HR 或薪酬团队记录已批准的加班。

## Control Requirement / 控制要求


Unapproved overtime should not be used as the basis for compensation claims.
未经批准的加班不应作为补偿申请依据。

## Eligibility / 适用条件

Overtime must be pre-approved unless required for an emergency incident.
除紧急事件外，加班必须事先获得批准。
