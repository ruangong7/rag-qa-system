# Sanctions Screening / 制裁筛查

## Objective / 目标

Sanctions screening helps prevent business dealings with prohibited parties.
制裁筛查用于防止与受限制方开展业务。

## Ownership / 责任归属

The business sponsor initiates screening, and Compliance reviews flagged matches.
业务发起人负责触发筛查，合规团队负责审核命中结果。

## Screening Points / 筛查节点

- New customer onboarding
- 新客户准入
- New vendor onboarding
- 新供应商准入
- Material contract renewal
- 重大合同续签
