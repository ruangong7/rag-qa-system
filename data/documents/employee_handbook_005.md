# Parental Leave

## Eligibility


Parental leave applies to eligible full-time employees in accordance with local labor law and company policy.

## HR Administration


HR records the leave dates, validates eligibility, and coordinates payroll handling where required.

## Important Note


Employees should notify their manager and HR as early as possible to support workload planning and handover.

## Leave Entitlements


- Maternity leave: 98 calendar days
- Paternity leave: 15 calendar days
