# Annual Leave Carryover

## Approval and Audit Trail

- The manager must verify the employee's remaining leave balance before approval.
- The HRBP must record the approved carryover days in Workday and ensure the data is accurate.

## Employee Procedure


1. Submit a carryover request through the Feishu HR system before the end of the current year.

## FAQ


**Q: When should I apply for annual leave carryover?**
A: You should submit the request before the current calendar year ends.

**Q: What happens if I have more than 5 unused days?**
A: Only 5 days may be carried over. Any excess balance expires under the policy.

**Q: Can I use the carried-over leave at any time next year?**
A: Yes, as long as the leave is fully used within the next calendar year.

**Q: What if my manager does not approve in time?**
A: Contact your manager first. If the delay threatens the year-end deadline, escalate to your HRBP.

## Fact Anchors

## Policy Rules


Under company policy, unused annual leave may be carried over into the next year, subject to the following rules:
- Each employee may carry over a maximum of 5 days of annual leave.
- Carried-over leave must be used within the next calendar year.

## Scope


This policy applies to all full-time employees. It does not apply to vendor onboarding workflows or customer ticket routing procedures.
