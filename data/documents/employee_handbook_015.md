# Employee Referral / 员工内推

## Exclusions / 不适用范围

Executive hires and agency-sourced candidates may be excluded.
高管招聘和猎头来源候选人可能不适用本政策。

## Key Rules / 关键规则

- Referrals must be submitted through the official recruiting channel.
- 内推必须通过正式招聘渠道提交。
- Hiring decisions remain independent from the referral relationship.
- 录用决策必须独立于推荐关系。
- Referral bonuses are paid only after the candidate passes the defined retention period.
- 内推奖金仅在候选人通过规定留任期后发放。

## Program Goal / 项目目标

The referral program encourages employees to recommend qualified external candidates.
内推项目鼓励员工推荐合格的外部候选人。
