# Attendance Management / 考勤管理

## Core Rules / 核心规则


- Late arrival and early departure must be recorded in the attendance system.
- 迟到和早退必须在考勤系统中记录。
- Missing attendance records require manager confirmation.
- 缺失的考勤记录需要经理确认。
- Repeated violations may lead to disciplinary action.
- 多次违规可能导致纪律处分。

## Policy Summary / 政策摘要


Employees must record attendance accurately and comply with working hour requirements.
员工必须如实记录考勤，并遵守工作时间要求。

## System of Record / 记录系统

Attendance data is maintained in the HR attendance platform.
考勤数据维护在 HR 考勤平台中。
