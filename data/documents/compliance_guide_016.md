# Sensitive Personal Data / 敏感个人信息

## Additional Controls / 附加控制

Processing sensitive personal data may require stricter access controls, explicit consent, and enhanced logging.
处理敏感个人信息可能需要更严格的访问控制、单独同意和增强日志。

## Common Categories / 常见类别

- Government identifiers
- 政府身份证件信息
- Financial account details
- 金融账户信息
- Health and medical information
- 健康与医疗信息
- Biometric data
- 生物识别信息

## Definition / 定义

Sensitive personal data includes information that could cause significant harm if misused or disclosed.
敏感个人信息指一旦被滥用或泄露可能造成重大损害的信息。
