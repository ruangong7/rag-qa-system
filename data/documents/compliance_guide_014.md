# Incident Escalation / 事件升级

## Escalation Triggers / 升级触发条件

- Potential data breach
- 潜在数据泄露
- Regulatory reporting requirement
- 存在监管报告义务
- Major service disruption involving sensitive data
- 涉及敏感数据的重大服务中断

## Purpose / 目的

This procedure defines how security and compliance incidents are escalated.
本流程定义安全与合规事件的升级方式。

## Response Standard / 响应标准

Critical incidents must be escalated immediately to the designated response team and executive stakeholders.
关键事件必须立即升级至指定响应团队和管理层干系人。
