# Mandatory Training

## Completion Timeline

New hires must complete assigned mandatory training within 30 calendar days of joining unless a different deadline is specified.

## Consequence of Non-Completion

Failure to complete mandatory training may result in restricted system access or performance follow-up.

## Monitoring


Managers and HRBP teams review completion reports regularly and escalate overdue items.

## Policy Statement


All employees must complete mandatory training assigned by HR, Legal, Security, or their functional department.

## Required Topics


- Information security awareness
- Data protection and privacy
- Code of conduct
- Anti-harassment and workplace ethics
