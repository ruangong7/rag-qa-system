# Employee Handbook — 员工手册 v3.2

## Chapter 1: Company Overview / 公司概况

### 1.1 Mission & Values / 使命与价值观
Our mission is to deliver innovative enterprise solutions that empower organizations worldwide.
我们的使命是提供创新的企业级解决方案，赋能全球组织。

Core Values / 核心价值观:
- Integrity / 诚信: We act with honesty and transparency in all interactions.
- Innovation / 创新: We embrace change and pursue continuous improvement.
- Collaboration / 协作: We believe the best results come from diverse teams working together.

### 1.2 Organizational Structure / 组织架构
The company operates under a flat hierarchy with cross-functional teams organized by product lines.
公司采用扁平化管理结构，按产品线组织跨职能团队。

## Chapter 2: Employment Policies / 雇佣政策

### 2.1 Working Hours / 工作时间
Standard working hours: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (Beijing Time, UTC+8).
标准工作时间：周一至周五，上午 9:00 至下午 6:00（北京时间 UTC+8）。

Flexible work arrangement: Employees may apply for flexible hours with manager approval.
弹性工作安排：员工可在经理批准后申请弹性工时。

### 2.2 Remote Work Policy / 远程办公政策
Employees are eligible for up to 3 days of remote work per week after completing probation.
员工在试用期结束后，每周最多可申请 3 天远程办公。

Remote work requires stable internet connection and availability during core hours (10:00-12:00, 14:00-17:00).
远程办公需确保网络稳定，并在核心工作时间（上午 10:00-12:00，下午 14:00-17:00）保持在线。

## Chapter 3: Leave & Benefits / 假期与福利

### 3.1 Annual Leave / 年假制度
- Junior level (<3 years): 5 working days per year
- Mid level (3-10 years): 10 working days per year
- Senior level (>10 years): 15 working days per year

- 初级员工（<3 年）：每年 5 个工作日
- 中级员工（3-10 年）：每年 10 个工作日  
- 高级员工（>10 年）：每年 15 个工作日

Unused annual leave may be carried over to the next calendar year, up to a maximum of 5 days.
未使用的年假可顺延至下一日历年，最多 5 天。

### 3.2 Sick Leave / 病假
Employees are entitled to 5 days of paid sick leave per year with medical certificate.
员工每年享有 5 天带薪病假，需提供医疗证明。

### 3.3 Parental Leave / 育儿假
- Maternity leave: 98 days (in compliance with PRC Labor Law)
- Paternity leave: 15 days
- 产假：98 天（符合中国劳动法）
- 陪产假：15 天

## Chapter 4: Code of Conduct / 行为准则

### 4.1 Confidentiality / 保密义务
All employees must protect confidential information including but not limited to:
- Trade secrets and proprietary technology
- Financial data and business plans
- Personal data of employees and customers
- Source code and technical documentation

全体员工必须保护机密信息，包括但不限于：
- 商业机密和专有技术
- 财务数据和商业计划
- 员工和客户的个人数据
- 源代码和技术文档

Violation of confidentiality may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
违反保密义务可能导致纪律处分，最高可至解除劳动合同。

### 4.2 Data Classification / 数据分类
All company data must be classified into one of four levels:
1. Public (公开): Information approved for public release
2. Internal (内部): For employees and authorized contractors only
3. Confidential (机密): Restricted to specific teams or roles
4. Restricted (绝密): Limited to named individuals with explicit authorization

## Chapter 5: IT & Security / 信息技术与安全

### 5.1 Acceptable Use Policy / 可接受使用政策
Company devices and networks must not be used for:
- Unauthorized access to systems or data
- Distribution of malicious software
- Activities that violate applicable laws and regulations
- Excessive personal use that impacts work performance

### 5.2 Password Policy / 密码策略
- Minimum 12 characters
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, digit, and special character
- Password rotation every 90 days
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) mandatory for all systems
