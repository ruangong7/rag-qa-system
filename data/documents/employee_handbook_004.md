# Core Collaboration Hours

## Employee Expectations


- Remain available on approved communication channels during core collaboration hours.
- Schedule internal meetings inside these windows whenever practical.
- Notify the manager in advance if medical appointments or approved leave affect availability.

## Exceptions


Employees on approved leave, field travel, or customer-facing emergency duty may be exempt for the affected period.

## Manager Responsibilities


- Use core collaboration hours as the default meeting window for cross-functional coordination.
- Avoid recurring meetings outside the standard window unless justified by time-zone overlap.

## Policy Summary


Core collaboration hours are the time blocks during which all team members are expected to be reachable for synchronous work.

## Standard Hours


- Morning collaboration window: 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM Beijing Time
- Afternoon collaboration window: 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM Beijing Time
